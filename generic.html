Our Commitment

Our team of board-certified music therapists specializes in working with children and teens with autism and related disabilities…and we truly LOVE what we do! We provide services throughout Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties in Florida, and nationwide via online. Our core values drive us to use music to enrich lives, celebrate differences, provide growth opportunities, uphold integrity, and ensure lasting impact.
