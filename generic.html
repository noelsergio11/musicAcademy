Who are we?

“Empowering every child through sound and soul.”

Our Founders

BRIGHT TUNE ACADEMY LLC WAS CREATED BY Ms. MARIA RODRIGUEZ AND Mr. SERGIO MORENO WITH A VISION TO EMPOWER CHILDREN OF ALL ABILITIES THROUGH THE TRANSFORMATIVE POWER OF MUSIC. At Bright Tune Academy, we believe that every child has a unique rhythm waiting to be discovered. Our team of passionate music educators and therapists specialize in working with children on the autism spectrum and with developmental differences, using music as a bridge for growth, connection, and joy. Through personalized sessions and inclusive programs, we nurture creativity, confidence, and communication — ensuring every note helps shape a brighter future.

Our Commitment

Our team of board-certified music therapists specializes in working with children and teens with autism and related disabilities…and we truly LOVE what we do! We provide services throughout Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties in Florida, and nationwide via online. Our core values drive us to use music to enrich lives, celebrate differences, provide growth opportunities, uphold integrity, and ensure lasting impact.

Why choose BTune Academy LLC?

  • Inclusive programs: For all abilities and ages, including autism.
  • Expert instructors: Certified and passionate educators.
  • Confidence-building: Through music, creativity, and connection.
  • Flexible access: Virtual and in-person options nationwide.
